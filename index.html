Personal Information

The personal information section gives employers information about who you are and how they can contact you. If you are coming up with a CV Manually, include your emails, GitHub and LinkedIn address as hyper-text. This makes it easier for your employer to check out your profiles or email you, as they simply have to click on the links, instead of copying and pasting in a browser tab.
